About us

Amstel Academy was created with the desire of making outstanding corporate training accessible to small, mid-size and large corporations alike.
The success of our programs lies on the fact that they are designed with practical outcomes in mind and ways to measure them.
Amstel Academy strives to ensure its course materials and instructors are the best available anywhere. Our team has a proven track-record of success, years of experience and is highly-skilled at bringing practical, real-world experience in an engaging and relevant way.
Backed by a solid network of educators and instructional designers, Amstel Academy also offers several corporate training options that are sure to equip your team with the best tools available.
Amstel Academy is uniquely qualified to support your organization with quality training solutions and is a one-stop shop for your training and Learning Management System needs.
